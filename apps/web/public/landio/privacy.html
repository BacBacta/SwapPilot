Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to SwapPilot ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our decentralized application (dApp) and related services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any information you provide while using our platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Blockchain Data

When you use SwapPilot, we interact with public blockchain networks. Please note that blockchain transactions are public by nature. We may collect:

  • Wallet addresses used to connect to our platform
  • Transaction history related to swaps executed through our service
  • Token balances and approval data

2.2 Technical Data

We automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our platform:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our platform
  • Referral source

2.3 Information You Provide

If you contact us or participate in surveys, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Communication content
  • Feedback and survey responses

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: To execute token swaps, display quotes, and facilitate transactions
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Communication: To respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party providers who help us operate our platform (e.g., analytics providers)
  • Blockchain Networks: Transaction data is inherently public on blockchain networks
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with various third-party services:

  • DEX Protocols: PancakeSwap, 1inch, ParaSwap, and others
  • Wallet Providers: MetaMask, Trust Wallet, and WalletConnect-compatible wallets
  • Analytics: We use privacy-respecting analytics tools

Each of these services has its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Blockchain data is permanent and cannot be deleted due to the immutable nature of distributed ledgers.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure coding practices

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (where technically feasible)
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@swappilot.io

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies necessary for the functioning of our platform:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality (e.g., wallet connection state)
  • Preference Cookies: Store your settings and preferences (e.g., dark mode)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

10. Children's Privacy

SwapPilot is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

As a decentralized application, our services are accessible globally. Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, where data protection laws may differ.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@swappilot.io
  • Discord: discord.gg/swappilot
  • Twitter: @SwapPilotDeFi
